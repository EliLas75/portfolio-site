Business Challenge & Strategic Solution

Business Challenges

  • Forecast Accuracy: Low reliability of existing demand forecasting models
  • Global Visibility: Limited transparency across international subsidiaries
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Strategic decisions made without consolidated analytics
  • Manual Overhead: Time-intensive processes prone to human error
  • Reactive Management: Absence of proactive stockout prevention alerts
  • Data Fragmentation: Critical information scattered across multiple systems

Strategic Solution Delivered

  • Kinaxis Integration: Direct connection to enterprise planning system
  • Real-time BI: Interactive Power BI dashboards with live data
  • Intelligent Segmentation: Dynamic ABC/XYZ classification algorithms
  • Custom KPIs: Forecast accuracy, availability rates, and bias metrics
  • Automated Alerting: Power Automate-driven proactive notifications
  • Centralized Repository: Unified data hub with complete historization

Core Capabilities & Features

Executive Command Center

Unified Power BI interface delivering real-time KPIs, interactive visualizations, and multi-dimensional drill-down capabilities across subsidiaries and product lines.

AI-Driven Product Segmentation

Advanced ABC (revenue contribution) and XYZ (demand variability) classification algorithms for optimized inventory strategy and resource allocation.

Predictive Analytics Engine

Sophisticated temporal analysis including trend forecasting, seasonality detection, and demand pattern recognition for enhanced prediction accuracy.

Enterprise Data Governance

Comprehensive historical data extraction and storage system with shared Excel repositories, version control, and complete audit trail capabilities.
